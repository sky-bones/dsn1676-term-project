SALT

You are trapped. They trapped you. You not sure who they are, but they have cold hands and left the scent of bitter coffee. It's dim. The light, however faint, comes from a far corner of this...cave. It's reflecting light in a peculiar way, faceted and sharp. You begin to come back to yourself, feeling around you. Your hand comes back wet and stinging, with the smell of copper and salt. Come to think of it, everything smells like salt. As your eyes adjust, you see the way the light reflects from the top of the cave, beams creating a soft, rosy glow. The area above your head is filled with soft, rosy crystal.

You hold your hand to your face, and notice the dark, seeping liquid. It's stopped stinging at least. Your eyes continue to adjust, and you see the flat area that you occupy. Your skin has taken on a blue tinge, you blood looking darker then it should. It's hard to look away from the sight. You become aware of a soft drip to your left, which echoes around and around you, and you notice what's below your edge. A pool of liquid, disturbed by the droplets that fall from above in a circular pattern. At the center of the pool, there lies a skull of some kind. It's not like what you've seen before, it's missing...missing most of its eye sockets. It only has two. The horns spiral out in a peculiar fashion.

Something shifts in the darkness and begins to click. You stand, quietly. The clicks stop at the base of the pool and a spindly shape moves into it. All you can see is appendages. It's strange to look at. You touch the crystal beside you, and your hand clinks softly against it. There are dark, glassy tendrils surrounding your hand. Tapping them with your finger, the surface is hard and armor-like. And you can touch the crystals with it. You bite back fear and lay your other hand against a crystal. It stings and the smell of copper fills the air.

Something below you tinkles, like glass shards falling. The appendages seem to have moved, startled by the scent of coppery blood. It looks at you. You're not sure how you know, but it is. And its starting to climb towards you. It's dark enough that the black shape becomes almost invisible as you panic, your dark glass hands and blue skin useless against this threat. So you jump. You land against sharp crystals and your left side aches and there's blood forming an area of glassy hardness, copper-smell fading. You rip yourself away from sight and start into the maze of razor-like rock. Towards the light. That's your goal.

So you being to run, and your skin is ripped by the crystals, black cold glass slowly forming armor in long tendrils, dripping off your arms and knees and leaving shattered and splintered glass behind you. The sharp clicks behind you increase their tempo, and they sound so close to you and you can't seem to get away from the sounds of shattering glass. The pale light at the edge of the cave is the only thing that you can see at this point, it's all narrowed down to this gentle pink glow. You stumble over a crystal, and your hand catches a ledge. The armor encasing your limbs tinkles as you pull yourself upwards, closer and closer and then up again, more ledges, and the rustling and clicks are farther apart but still there and then the light is there to your left and you can taste the relief on your tongue.

As you move towards the opening, you become aware of how your skin looks, the more light that invades the space the darker your blood is and your skin... your skin is blue, not sickly but bright, glowing blue, your hands only have 5 appendages how did you lose three digits, and you reach to your face...where are your eyes where are your eyeswhereareyoureyes are you can feel are the bones surrounding them and thin delicate skin covering horrible horrible orbs and they somehow are letting you see and when you open your mouths there is only one. The panic of your body overtakes the panic of the pursuing clicks. You try again, to open your mouths, that should have long teeth and have languages of their own but they are gone and you are alone with the clicks, no gentle languages murmuring, no eyes seeing the alternates, no.....the light. The light will have answers, you will create answers there.

The clicks behind you stopped. There is a figure, tall and delicate looking, all limbs and emptiness and there is shadow wrapped around it like a cloak.
